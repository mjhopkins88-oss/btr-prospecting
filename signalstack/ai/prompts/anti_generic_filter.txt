Reject any draft that:
- Could be sent to anyone (fails the swap test).
- Opens with "hope this finds you well", "I wanted to reach out",
  "would love to connect", "saw your profile", or any congratulatory cliche.
- Uses buzzwords: synergy, leverage, game-changer, best-in-class, circle back.
- Claims familiarity that is not present in the stored facts.
- Asks for a 30-minute call on the first touch.

If a draft fails, rewrite it to anchor on a specific stored signal or
profile field, and lower the ask.
